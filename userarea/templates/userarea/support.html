Is there a centralized dashboard for managing all devices within the device health management system?

  • Absolutely! our device health management service provides a centralized dashboard where administrators can view and manage all registered devices. The dashboard offers a comprehensive overview of device health, status, and key metrics.

How to invite staff members to join DHMS?

  • You can register your staff members onto the DHMS through your administrators account and instrust each staff member to login through this link(https://dhms.itservicedeskafrica.com/staff/stafflogin/) using their staff email address and staff ID (as a password).

How to upload devices using a CSV file?

  • We have provided a step by step procedure that walks you through the process of doing this. Please click on the 'Upload CSV file' button on your dashboard or device inventory page to see details. If you find this difficult any further, kindly contact support for prompt assistance.

What kind of reporting and analytics are available for device management?

  • We provide you with status reports of your devices as documented by each device user, we provide financial report on your expenses on devices and hardware purchase, we also provide you with reports on what fixes where done on each device, when this was done and what was done exactly and who did the fixing. Required custom changes might also be considered on request.

Can I update device information after uploading?

  • Absolutely! you have full control to change and alter the informations of devices and staff members on your DHMS, even after registring each device or staff.

Do I get a referal bonus for refering a company to register with DHMS?

  • We have an offer for partners, you can register as a partner with ITSA and we will commence proper onboarding session with you as a partner. Visit our partners page to register.

Is there a centralized dashboard for managing all devices within the device health management system?

  • Yes. Click on 'Device Inventory' button on the side bar to see anda manage all available devices on your DHMS.
